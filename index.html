About Us

The African Safari Zoo is a unique zoo dedicated to its animals! Our enclosures are large and mimic a real African safari which allows our animals to roam freely and comfortably. These enclosures also create an immersive experience for our visitors. We are confident that our zoo is a zoo unlike any other and the entire family will have a great time exploring all of our lovely animals! We also have a playground for younger kids and lots of restaraunts for when the family needs a break from all the fun.
